Floating Balls Demo

Interactive physics-based skill visualization for modern portfolios

How to Interact

  • Drag: Click and drag balls to throw them around
  • Click: Click on any ball to see a confetti effect and give it a random impulse
  • Hover: Hover over balls to see the category tooltip and glow effect
  • Reset: Click the "Reset" button to return balls to random starting positions
  • Shuffle: Click "Shuffle" to give all balls random velocities
  • Watch: Just watch them bounce and interact with each other!
